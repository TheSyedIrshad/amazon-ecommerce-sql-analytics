# Business Questions & Analytics Intent

This document captures the business questions that guided the analytics design.

---

## Revenue & Growth

1. What is total revenue, and how is it distributed across product categories?
2. How does revenue trend over time (monthly)?

---

## Customer Behavior

3. Who are repeat customers, and how many orders do they place?
4. Who are the top customers by total spend?
5. How concentrated is revenue among top customers?

---

## Order Economics

6. What is the Average Order Value (AOV)?
7. How does order value vary across time?

---

## Product Performance

8. Which products generate the most revenue?
9. Are there products with high revenue but low volume (pricing insight)?

---

## Payments & Operations

10. Which payment methods are most used?
11. How much revenue does each payment method contribute?

---

## Notes

- All questions are answered using SQL with correct grain handling
- Window functions are used only where they add analytical value
- This list reflects real analytics questions asked in e-commerce businesses
